What is Energy Conservation?

Energy conservation is a decision and practice of using less energy. Energy conservation is a matter of utter importance in today's world. Energy conservation is not about making limited resources last as long as possible , that would mean that you are doing nothing more than prolonged crisis until you finally run out of energy resources.

Conservation is a process of reducing demand on a limited supply and enabling that supply to begin to rebuild itself. If humans doesnt conserve energy,the world will depelete its natural resources. The goal of energy conservation techniques is Protect and replenish supplies , develop and use alterative energy sources , and to clean up the damage from the prior energy processes.
