Services

The product focuses on controlling the unecessary usage of lighting systems. It detects the number of people present at an instant and then controls the lighting requirements accordingly. Smart Lighting system works on the principal that they will light up when there are people and will dim-out when there is no one. In the study area on campus , the AI technology will analyze if any student is sitting there and will make the light above the study table bright and will make it dim if no one is there by using daylight sensor, motion sensor , location sensor and camera vision. Camera vision is powered by article AI which recognizes the number of students

In outdoor places, the light systems will be equipped with daylight sensors which will turn off the lights when there is no sufficient sunlight. The smart lights will sense the number of people at a place and will make the light brighter only at that particular area using GPS, motion sensor and computer vision.

What Is Our PLAN?

Our product is fully equipped to work in all the conditions. It will take information from the cityu app which will ask the user to provide permission for gps. This is required for the analysis of the number of people in the area and also someone is sitting in the study area. If there is no motion for 15 mins the lights will slowly start to dim-off.
